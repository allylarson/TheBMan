Our Services

Coast to Coast On Site Service

We travel from coast to coast providing on site service of your Bartelt® or Pouchmaster packaging machines.

Analyze

From evaluating your maintenance program, troubleshooting mechanical issues, troubleshooting film problems or helping you diagnose electrical problems, we can get your machines up and running!

Refurbish

We can refurbish your existing packager to any extent your budget allows. This also includes your safety guarding. See our Machinery page for details.

Inspection and Evaluation

Buying a used packager from a third party? We can do an inspection and evaluation for you, potentially saving you thousands in repairs, down time and late product delivery to your customers!

Service You Expect

At The B Man we take our business seriously and strive to provide the best service available. With over 20 years of service experience, we know the equipment like the back of our hand.

Specializing in Bartelt® and HMC Pouchmaster horizontal form fill and seal packagers, we will do everything we can to get your machines running properly and help you improve your business offerings!

Coast to Coast Support

We offer extensive on site training, from coast to coast! We will come to you to ensure that your team has the knowledge they need to effectively man the equipment.

We will also do our best to improve and increase your efficiency by ensuring performance is top notch!

About Us

The B Man specializes in Bartelt® and HMC Pouchmaster horizontal form fill and seal packagers since 1991. We offer over 20 years of experience and are committed to providing the service and training you need!
